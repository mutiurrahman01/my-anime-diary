---

# Product Vision

My Anime Diary is a private anime tracking platform.

It is NOT:

- Social Media
- Anime Streaming Website
- Anime Download Website
- Anime News Website

The purpose is simple.

Users privately maintain their own anime diary.

Everything belongs only to the owner.

No one else can see it.

---

# Problem Statement

Anime fans watch hundreds of anime.

After some years they usually forget

- what they watched
- what rating they gave
- which anime became their favorite
- when they watched it

Current platforms are feature rich and include social features.

This project focuses on a private and distraction-free experience.
